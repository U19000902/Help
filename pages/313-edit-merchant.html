3.1.3 Edit Merchant

Edit Merchant Screen

Purpose: The owner should be allowed to update the merchant’ details that they have selected from the view merchant section. The view will show all the merchant in rows and can be ordered by the various merchant details. 

 

No.

Item Name

Description

1.             

Merchant Name textbox

The product owner will click here to update merchant name

2.

Email Address textbox

The product owner will click here to update the email address.

3.

Contact Details textbox

The product owner will click here to update contact number

4.

Status dropdown list

The product owner can change status from active to archived.

5.

Street Address textbox

The product owner can click here to update the merchants street address.

6.

Update merchant button

The product owner can click this button to update the merchant.

 

Success message:

7.

Back button

The product owner can click this to return to merchant home screen.
