3.14.4 Generate Shift Report

Purpose: This screen will display the shift report screen. The system will generate the shift report based on the data that is already in the system. The data that is to generate this report will be the employees name, the employees surname, the hours that they worked, when they logged in and out of a shift and the total amount of hours worked.
